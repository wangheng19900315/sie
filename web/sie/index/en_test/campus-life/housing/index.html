East China Normal University

The on-campus housing availability is limited at ECNU, so students are highly recommended to register and pay off the tuition and housing fee as early as possible since the room is first come first served.

a. On-campus housing:

We provide one on-campus housing choice in Shanghai: International Exchange Service Center. The rooms will be allocated to students randomly.

The International Exchange Service Center is an on-campus hotel run by Shanghai Hua-Shen Sino-Foreign Cultural Exchange Service Co., Ltd. directly under the supervision of East China Normal University. There are 255 rooms in the complex, including 71 economy rooms, 170 standard rooms and 14 suites. There are also entertainment facilities such as a coffee bar with an elegant and comfortable environment.
