College Students Abroad

Top-notch professors bring you extraordinary experience.

If you are currently an undergraduate in North American universities, our summer school programs will provide invaluable opportunities for you to:

  • Enjoy your summer in China while getting credits to be transferred to your home institution
  • Save more than $1,500 for tuition fees and living costs with an early graduation
  • Interact actively with professors in small-class lectures
  • Network with and obtain career guidance from successful entrepreneurs, industrial veterans and business elites
  • Meet thousands of your peers from over 200 colleges in North America and expand your social circle
  • Immerse in the diverse and vigorous cultures of Beijing and Shanghai during your stay
