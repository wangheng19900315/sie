About BJTU

BEIJING
BJTU ISS
Beijing Jiaotong University International Summer Session

Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU, formerly known as Northern Jiaotong University) is a national key university under the direct administration of the Ministry of Education and now is jointly supported by the Ministry of Education, the China Railway Corporation (formerly known as the Ministry of Railways) and Beijing Municipal Government. BJTU is one of the first universities selected into the “National 211 Project” and the “985 Strength Discipline Innovation Platform” project, one of the first institutions authorized to confer master’s and doctoral degrees. BJTU, as the leading organization, has established the Collaborative Innovation Centre for Rail Transit Safety, which is one of the first 14 collaborative centers approved by the Chinese government to enter the “National 2011 Projects”. 

About BJTU ISS

The BJTU International Summer Session (BJTU ISS), a member of SIE Alliance, is cooperatively run by Beijing Jiaotong University and Sinoway International Education. In 2017, we will maintain the high quality of our program and bring excellent summer session experience to our students.

Academic Information

In 2017, BJTU ISS will open a 4-week program, which offers a wide range of courses including Political Science, English, History, Accounting, Mathematics, Philosophy, Psychology, Economics, Sociology, Art History, etc. (Course List ). BJTU ISS's faculty team consists of experienced professors from many prestigious U.S. universities and colleges including Case Western Reserve University, UC Davis, and University of Michigen etc. 

Student who is applying for BJTU ISS 4-week session can choose up to two courses. Each course has 54 instruction hours (46 contact hours and 8 office hours)and will be awarded 4 credits, equivalent to a semester-long course in U.S. colleges. The number of credits that can be transferred to applicants' home universities in the states is subject to the registrar office of each university. Both BJTU students and non-BJTU students can apply for BJTU ISS.

Program Highlights

• Social Events

BJTU ISS social events offer a great platform to build up your social networks and provide you opportunities to exchange ideas with thousands of students and eminent professors from all over the world.

BJTU ISS has successfully organized many extracurricular activities last year, such as basketball games, board games and KTV night.

These activities were highly appreciated by SIE alumni. They are well beyond your imagination, and most importantly, they are all free!

• Business Program

Business Program (BP) of SIE Alliance is a high-end project, which aims to assist students on their career path. Every year, students learn a lot in their summer sessions by attending hosting lectures and networking sessions,  even being offered internship opportunities in banks, accounting agencies, consulting companies, law offices, private equity funds and the IT industry.

In the past years, we have invited many successful business leaders and outstanding graduates to give lectures and advice to the students. With the opportunities provided by BP, SIE students are able to familiarize themselves with business operation in China, and acquire the necessary skills for job hunting.

These activities were highly appreciated by SIE alumni. They are well beyond your imagination, and most importantly, they are all free!
