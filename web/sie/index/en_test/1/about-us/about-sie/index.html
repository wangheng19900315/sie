About SIE

Headquartered in Beijing and founded in 2009, Sinoway International Education Group (SIE) is the largest U.S. college-level education provider and the first to operate accredited American-style summer sessions in China.

Over the past several years, SIE has become a leading educational company in Asia with over 8000 students and 250 faculties having participated in our program.

With a multilingual knowledgeable team comprised of professionals with overseas education background, SIE has a deep understanding of the needs of students in the states and the academic standard of U.S. college education.

From their unique perspective, the SIE staffs are ready and able to provide students with an unforgettable summer experience and an invaluable platform which will benefit them in terms of academics, social network and career.

2015

Since 2009, over 8,000 students have participated in SIE International Summer Sessions.

2014

SIE started free trial courses for the first time, in collaboration with Beijing Normal University and East China Normal University. More events were held in order to keep students and American professors in close contact.

2013

SIE began partnering with Beijing Normal University to open its first four-week International Summer Session.

Since 2013, SIE program has been verified by CDGDC.

2012

SIE assisted Nanjing University and Sun Yat-sen University with establishing International Summer Sessions.

More than 2,000 students enrolled in SIE’s International Summer Sessions, increasing the cumulative number of students to over 3,200.

2011

SIE entered into an exclusive strategic alliance with Renren Incorporated (NYSE: RENN), the largest social networking service in China, expanding its reach to millions of users.

University of International Business and Economics (UIBE) collaborated with SIE to start its International Summer Session, creating the SIE Alliance of International Summer Sessions.

More than 1,000 students joined the International Summer Sessions of SIE Alliance, bringing the total number of SIE Alliance students to 1,200.

2010

SIE Business Program was founded.

SIE started hosting social events for our students.

234 students from over 30 universities enrolled in ECNU International Summer Sessions.

2009

Sinoway International Education (SIE) was established.

SIE began collaborating with East China Normal University (ECNU) in Shanghai launching the first ECNU International Summer Session.
