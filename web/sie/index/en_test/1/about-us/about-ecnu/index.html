About ECNU

SHANGHAI
ECNU ISS
East China Normal University International Summer Session

On October 16, 1951, East China Normal University (ECNU) was established out of the Great China University and Kwang Hua University’s arts and science faculties, while incorporating some other departments from Fudan University and Tongji University, etc. In March 1959, ECNU was officially recognized as one of the 16 key universities in China, and this status was reaffirmed in 1978.

Being a university with an international perspective, ECNU attaches great importance to the internationalization of its development and has established strategic cooperative partnerships with many world-renowned universities from all over the world. In 2008, it set up the Study and Training Base for International Chinese Teachers under Hanban, the National Office for Teaching Chinese as a Foreign Language. In 2011, the ground breaking of Shanghai New York University, a joint university established by ECNU and New York University in Shanghai, showed a new phase of ECNU’s internationalization plans.

Through decades of teaching and research practice, the university’s motto of “Seek Truth, Foster Originality, and Honor the Teaching Profession” has inspired generations of ECNU teachers and students to strive hard for the development of China’s basic education and the prosperity of the country. Now, ECNU is steadily and enthusiastically working towards its goal of establishing itself as an internationally renowned high-level research university, and will therefore play a more prominent role in China’s rise in the 21st century.

About ECNU ISS

The ECNU International Summer Session (ECNU ISS), a member of SIE Alliance which is the first and largest alliance of international summer sessions in Asia, is the first accredited American-style summer school program in China. Since 2010, the program has served over 2500 students from all over the world and has become the most successful summer school program in China. In 2016, we will continue to maintain the premium quality of our program and bring excellent summer session experience to our students.

Academic Information

The ECNU ISS transcripts are issued by ECNU Academic Affairs Office and all courses have been incorporated into the system of ECNU Academic Affairs Office. Each student applying for 4-week session can choose up to two courses, and for those who applying for 5-week session can choose up to three courses. Each course has 54 instruction hours (45 contact hours and 9 office hours) and will be awarded 4 credits by ECNU, equivalent to a semester-long course in U.S. colleges. The number of credits that can be transferred to applicants’ home universities in the states is subject to the registrar office of each university. Both ECNU students and non-ECNU students can apply for register in ECNU ISS. Both ECNU students and non-ECNU students can apply for ECNU ISS.

ECNU ISS will open two sessions, one is a 4-week program from May 30, 2016 to June 24, 2016 and the other one is a 5-week program from July 4, 2016 to August 6, 2016. Both sessions offer a wide range of courses including Political Science, English, History, Accounting, Mathematics, Philosophy, Psychology, Economics, Sociology, Art History and so on (Course List ). ECNU ISS’s faculty team consists of experienced professors from many prestigious U.S. universities including, Williams College, University of Virginia, etc.

Program Specialty

• Social Events

ECNU ISS social events offer a great platform to build your social networks and give you opportunities to exchange ideas with thousands of students and eminent professors from all over the world.

ECNU ISS has successfully organized many extracurricular activities in the last few years such as basketball games, board games and KTV night.

These activities were highly appreciated by the previous students. They are well beyond your imagination, and most importantly, they are all free!

• Business Program

Business Program (BP) of SIE Alliance is a high-end project, which aims to assist students on their career path. Every year, by attending hosting lectures, networking sessions, students learn a lot in their summer sessions, even being offered internship opportunities in banks, accounting agencies, consulting companies, law offices, private equity funds and the IT industry.

In the past years, we have invited many successful business leaders and outstanding graduates to give lectures and advice to the students. With the opportunities provided by BP, SIE students are able to familiarize themselves with business operation in China, and acquire the necessary skills for job hunting.

These activities were highly appreciated by the previous students. They are well beyond your imagination, and most importantly, they are all free!
