Eligibility

We welcome undergraduate students and high school seniors around the world to apply for our summer sessions. Limited high school students with strong academic record are also welcomed to apply for BJTU/ECNU ISS. BJTU/ECNU ISS admissions committee will evaluate their candidacy individually.

BJTU/ECNU ISS requires all applicants to prove their English language proficiency. Applicants must meet at least one of the following conditions:

a. Native English speaker

b. Studying at or graduated from an institution where English is the main language of instruction

c. With a GPA above 2.5 (2.5 not included)

d. Have a valid TOEFL (Test of English as a Foreign Language) score which is higher than 80 (iBT) or equivalent scores in other forms of TOEFL tests

e. Have a valid IELTS (International English Language Testing System) score which is higher than 6.0

f. Have passed CET-4 or other equivalent/higher level standard tests

Students who did not meet any condition above may also request a phone interview conducted in English by the BJTU/ECNU ISS recruiting officers, in lieu of providing examination scores. Successful completion of the interview will be sufficient for matriculation. The appointment for the phone interview can be made via email: iss@sieschool.org (for BJTU applicants) or iss@ied.ecnu.edu.cn (for ECNU applicant).
