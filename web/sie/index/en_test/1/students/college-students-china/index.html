BJTU undergraduate students register for the courses at the Teaching Affairs Office of BJTU and the credits earned will be counted as elective course credits. The office will release the application notification in every May, please follow the update of Teaching Affairs Office website.

2016 Enrollment information as follows:

Application requirements: For undergraduates who are interested in attending the summer courses, please check your course schedule and examination schedule in July. If your schedules have no conflict with the summer courses you want to attend and your final exams are in the form of quiz, you are eligible to sign up. We will accept your application on a first-come-first-served basis. The list of selected students will be announced on the Teaching Affairs Office’s website.

Registration: Applicants need to fill out the application form (Attachment) and paste your class schedule shots at the end of the form, and send the form to the Teaching Affairs Office’s mailbox by 11:30 on May 16. Please rename your email as "name + abbreviation of proposed courses". We will not accept any late entries.

ECNU undergraduate students register the courses at the Academic Affairs Office of ECNU and the credits earned will be counted as elective course credits. The office will release the application notification in every May, please follow the update of Academic Affairs Office website.

2016 Enrollment information as follows:

1. Undergraduates at East China Normal University are required to fill out the Application Form (attachment) and submit it to Ms. Dai at the Academic Affairs Office. Application Period of Validity: May 4th, 2016—May 15th, 2016.

2.Classes of the 4-week/5-week program are held from Monday to Friday . ECNU ISS offers 72 instruction hours for each course, and professors must hold office hours for two hours weekly.

3.Academic credits our students earn from ECNU International Summer Session are recognized and accepted as parts of their total credits they are required to graduate from ECNU.

4.Students who have passed the entrance examination are regulated by the academic rules of ECNU. Course Drop is not permitted; otherwise it can be reported as an exam absence.

5.Contact: Mr. Dai

Tel: (86)021-54531282

Add: Room 211, Administrative Building, Minhang Campus; Teaching Office, 2nd floor, Liberal Arts Building, North Zhongshan Campus

If you are currently an undergraduate in China and would like to pursue your postgraduate study in North American institutions, our high-end summer school programs will enable you to:

  • Fully experience an English teaching environment with authentic and unique North American educational features
  • Have the chance to get reference letters from the professors, which will greatly help with your postgraduate application
  • Acquire a better understanding of the life of studying abroad and be prepared
  • Enrich your knowledge while networking with thousands of outstanding peers and professors
  • Network with and obtain career guidance from successful entrepreneurs, industrial veterans and business elites
