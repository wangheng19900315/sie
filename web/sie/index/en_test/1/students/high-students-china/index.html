High School Students in China

To experience authentic North-American education in advance.

Selected high school students with proven English proficiency level and academic record can also apply for SIE International Summer Session.

Our Courses:

All SIE Summer Courses are instructed by qualified professors from universities in North-America and follow the same structure and standard of most U.S. Universities. SIE International Summer School offers courses in the field of Economics, Philosophy, literature,the science of law, history, science, and so on. These courses are not only obligatory courses in host universities but also correspond with the courses overseas. Students can choose 4-week or 5-week program which is instructed in English; each course in BJTU has 64 credit hours and each course in ECNU has 72 credit hours.

In addition, SIE also offer students opportunities to take part in social events and business programs to comprehensively improve their abilities in oversea living, academic communicating, critical thinking, international leadership, and network resource developing.

Advantages of our program:

1.If you are a high school student of senior one or two planning to study at university abroad, then through SIE ISS, you can achieve:

  • Experience classroom atmosphere of North-American Universities and adapt to English Teaching environment in advance.
  • Have a face to face communication with professors and oversea students in North America
  • Study 2 or 3 U.S. college-level courses and get the transcript
  • Get to know the different academic and living background of American universities.
  • Get to know application information of American colleges and universities
  • Opportunities to get recommendation letter from American college and university professors
  • Gain competitive advantage over your peers in college application

2. If you already have an offer, then before you start your study abroad, SIE ISS will enable you to:

  • Take advantage of the summer vacation to take 2-3 U.S. college-level courses, get the transcript and transfer credits back to the American schools
  • Earn credits in advance and save at most 2/3 of the cost
  • Meet famous professors from all over the world, business elites and nice schoolmates and plan for your future career systematically in advance.
