Beijing Jiaotong University International Summer Session

About BJTU ISS

The BJTU International Summer Session (BJTU ISS), a new member of SIE Alliance, is cooperated by Beijing Jiaotong University and SIE International Summer School. The SIE program has been successfully held for 5 years since 2011. Thousands of students from all over the world have joined the summer session.
In 2016, we will continue to maintain the premium quality of our program and bring excellent summer session experience to our students.


Academic Information

In 2016, BJTU ISS will open a 4-week program, which offers a wide range of courses including English, History, Accounting, Mathematics, Philosophy, Psychology, Economics, Sociology and Art History (Course List ). BJTU ISS's faculty team consists of experienced professors from many prestigious U.S. universities and colleges including Johns Hopkins University，University of Virginia, Vassar College etc.
Each student applying for BJTU ISS 4-week session can choose up to two courses. Each course has 54 instruction hours (45 contact hours and 9 office hours)and will be awarded 4 credits, equivalent to a semester-long course in U.S. colleges. The number of credits that can be transferred to applicants' home universities in the states is subject to the registrar office of each university. Both BJTU students and non-BJTU students can apply for BJTU ISS.

How to Apply

Please register for the program via-the Online Application System .

Program Specialty

• Social Events

SIE Alliance has successfully organized many extracurricular activities in the last few years such as theme parties, historical site tours, board games and KTV night. As an energetic new member, BJTU ISS will offer a great platform to build your social networks and provide you with opportunities to exchange ideas with thousands of students and eminent professors from all over the world.



• Business Program
Business Program (BP) of SIE Alliance is a high-end project, which aims to assist students on their career path. Every year, by attending hosting lectures, networking sessions, students learn a lot in their summer sessions, even being offered internship opportunities in banks, accounting agencies, consulting companies, law offices, private equity funds and the IT industry.
In the past years, we have invited many successful business leaders and outstanding graduates to give lectures and advice to the students. With the opportunities provided by BP, SIE students are able to familiarize themselves with business operation in China, and acquire the necessary skills for job hunting.
These activities were highly appreciated by the previous students. They are well beyond your imagination, and most importantly, they are all free!
