CDGDC Verification

CDGDC, standing for China Academic Degrees and Graduate Education Development Center, is an administrative department directly under the Ministry of Education, operating under the joint leadership of the Ministry of Education (MoE) and the Academic Degrees Committee of the State Council (ADCSC), and possessing the independent qualification of legal entity. CDGDC started to verify the Chinese degree certificate and other educational background since 2000. The higher education verification CDGDC conducts includes: all levels and kinds of degree certificates, transcripts, studying certificates and other background documentations issued by institution including higher education institutions and scientific research institutions that belong to national education system within mainland of China (exclude Hong Kong and Macau).

Currently, CDGDC is the only authority to verify the domestic higher education certificates and transcripts. It is also recognized by mainstream American education verification institutions, colleges and universities, especially for WES and ECE, who require student to do the CDGDC Verification first and then issue the final verification report after receiving the CDGDC Verification result.
