About ECNU ISS

The ECNU International Summer Session (ECNU ISS), a member of SIE Alliance which is the first and largest alliance of international summer sessions in Asia, is the first accredited American-style summer school program in China. Since 2010, the program has served over over 2500 students from all over the world and has become the most successful summer school program in China. In 2016, we will continue to maintain the premium quality of our program and bring excellent summer session experience to our students.

Academic Information

The ECNU ISS transcripts are issued by ECNU Academic Affairs Office and all the courses have been incorporated into the system of ECNU Academic Affairs Office. Each course has 54 instruction hours (46contact hours and 8 office hours) and will be awarded 4 credits by ECNU, equivalent to a semester-long course in U.S. colleges. The number of credits that can be transferred to applicants’ home universities in the states is subject to the registrar office of each university. Both ECNU students and non-ECNU students can apply for register in ECNU ISS. Both ECNU students and non-ECNU students can apply for ECNU ISS.
ECNU ISS will open two sessions, one is a 4-week program from May 30, 2016 to June 24, 2016 and the other one is a 5-week program from July 4, 2016 to August 6, 2016. Both sessions offer a wide range of courses including English, History, Accounting, Mathematics, Philosophy, Psychology, Economics, Sociology and Art History (Course List ). ECNU ISS’s faculty team consists of experienced professors from many prestigious U.S. universities including, Williams College, University of Virginia, etc.

How to Apply

Please register and login the Online Application System (ECNU undergraduate students register the courses at the Academic Affairs Office of ECNU. The office will release the application notification in every May, please follow the update of Academic Affairs Office website)

Program Specialty

• Social Events
ECNU ISS social events offer a great platform to build your social networks and give you opportunities to exchange ideas with thousands of students and eminent professors from all over the world.
ECNU ISS has successfully organized many extracurricular activities in the last few years such as basketball games, board games and KTV night.
These activities were highly appreciated by the previous students. They are well beyond your imagination, and most importantly, they are all free!



• Business Program
Business Program (BP) of SIE Alliance is a high-end project, which aims to assist students on their career path. Every year, by attending hosting lectures, networking sessions, students learn a lot in their summer sessions, even being offered internship opportunities in banks, accounting agencies, consulting companies, law offices, private equity funds and the IT industry.
In the past years, we have invited many successful business leaders and outstanding graduates to give lectures and advice to the students. With the opportunities provided by BP, SIE students are able to familiarize themselves with business operation in China, and acquire the necessary skills for job hunting.
These activities were highly appreciated by the previous students. They are well beyond your imagination, and most importantly, they are all free!
