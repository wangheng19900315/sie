Order Transcripts

Transcripts

ECNU/BJTU ISS will issue official transcript in both Chinese and English versions. The transcript will have detailed course information on it, including course title, Instructor, grade and credits for each course.

Sending Transcript

The official transcript will be sent to the address provided by student in the online application system within four to six weeks after the summer program ends. The sending period depends on whether the student offers complete and correct receiving address in time. If the student fails to provide the complete and correct address and result in our not able to send out, send out mistakenly or late or other consequences, the student shall bear all the responsibility and loss. Ordering second sending requires extra transcript and sending fees.

Providing Transcript Delivery Address

Please note: Most American Institutions require transcript directly sending back to the Academic Affairs Office, Undergraduate Office or Department Office. Therefore, we strongly recommend students to confirm the address with advisor or related department before coming to summer session. About how to fill out the complete and correct sending transcript address form, please refer to the instruction.

Extra Transcript

Every ECNU/BJTU ISS student is privileged to be awarded an official transcript delivered to the address provided on the application system for free. If you request more transcripts, the International Summer Session will charge a transcript fee and delivery fee.

Each additional copy of the official transcript costs 50 RMB (About $7.8), postage not included.

Students who requires extra transcript could pick up the transcript during the summer program at administration office, or contact bjtuiss@bjtu.edu.cn (for BJTU ISS students) or iss@ied.ecnu.edu.cn (for ECNU ISS students) after the program ends. Students should apply for extra transcript two weeks in advance and the timescale may be extended at particular busy times and holidays.
