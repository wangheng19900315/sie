Credit Transfer

Credit Transfer Guidance
STEP 1:

Make an appointment with your Academic Advisor, bring the syllabi of the courses you intend to take and tell him/her your plan for attending international summer sessions at East China Normal University/Beijing Jiaotong University. It is also highly recommended that you get positive confirmation in advance from the school or department to which you would like to transfer your credits.

Most likely, your Academic Advisor will evaluate the courses based on factors such as：

a.course format and content

b.syllabi

c.professor qualifications

d.class hours

You can easily find all the information by navigating our website (http://summer.sieschool.org). When in doubt, always feel free to contact our Student Service Department by email at bjtuiss@bjtu.edu.cn (for BJTU ISS program), or iss@ied.ecnu.edu.cn (for ECNU ISS program). We will be more than happy to help you with any question you may have.

If you are currently studying in domestic high school and haven’t been accepted by American schools yet, we strongly recommend you to talk about taking summer session courses with the academic officer or admission officer of the school you are applying for.

STEP 2:

After the evaluation, your advisor will assist you with all the necessary steps for successfully transferring credits back to your home institution. Most schools require students to complete a Transfer Credit Application Form and get it signed by the Academic Advisor. Others require an extra step which is to get a departmental approval. Your advisor or the department dean will decide what the equivalent course is for each course.

STEP 3:

Complete the online application and pay for the tuition and fees, and then you will be able to register for classes. Please make sure to fill out the correct mailing address for your transcript which you could confirm with the Registrar’s Office or Admission Office of your school.

STEP 4:

Upon the completion of the program, we will mail your transcript to the Admissions Office or Registrar office of your home institution in 4-6 weeks. You may request for your transcript to be mailed to other addresses as well.

STEP 5:

Make another appointment with your Academic Advisor to confirm courses have been successfully transferred to your home institution.

Important Note: The above information is only for reference. Your home institution reserves the final decision right for your credit transfer. Students must consult with the Study Abroad Office, Registrar’s Office, Advisor or any other credit-transfer related office or officer of your school about the detailed credit transfer procedures BEFORE you apply for BNU International Summer Session to make sure that the credits could be transferred back to your home institution successfully.
