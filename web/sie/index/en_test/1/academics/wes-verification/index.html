WES Verification

World Education Services (WES) is the largest credential evaluation institute in America, providing evaluations for those who intend to apply for jobs or admission to an educational institution in America or Canada. With a history of more than 40 years, WES’s evaluation reports are extensively accepted by academic institutions in these two countries. In 2009, China Academic Degrees and Graduate Education Development Center (CDGDC) established a partnership with WES. Since then, holders of diplomas and academic records in China (excluding Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan) shall firstly apply for evaluation reports of CDGDC before going to WES.

Here are the WES application guidance:

 
1
Application page

  • Vistit WES official websites http://www.wes.org

  • Click on 【Apply for a WES Credential Evaluation→】

  • Enter the following page:

 
2
Application materials

  • Choose China as your country of education in Step 1,
    then learn what documents you need to prepare.

 
3
Registration

  • Turn back to the first page, choose your destination in Step 2,
    and finish the registration.

 
4
Finish application

  • Finish application online;
    after that, you will receive a reference number;
    please keep it for your record.

Tips: When you fill the application form online, remember to choose course by course as your WES evaluation type.
