Student Union

In order to enrich after-class life in our summer school, SIE sets up Students Union. In Students Union you will assist our professors and students to make the school life easier, and meanwhile you can meet more friends and develop your connections. Whether you are our SIE campus representative, or simply university student who is interested in SIE, you are more than welcome to join.

What you need to know is that joining SIE Students Union would help you develop your connections with peer students, and also with world’s famous professors.

Below are some of our previous summer events. SIE Students Union will continue offering opportunities for similar events, and will innovate for our student. Welcome to join us!

  • Theme Party
  • Visiting historic spots
  • Basketball, bowling matches
  • Board games
  • KTV competition
  • Local events sharing
  • Prom
  • ……
